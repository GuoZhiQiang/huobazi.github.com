Email群发的时候：MassEmailingAdmin.aspx页面内插入表情和代码时，404错误

发现是Js文件内路径的问题
FTB-FreeTextBox.js内


 this.iPopup.document.location.href = "../emoticons.aspx";


改成


 this.iPopup.document.location.href = "/emoticons.aspx";


FTB-Pro.js内


 var codeWin = window.open("../insertcode.aspx","codeWin","width=530,height=420,status=0,toolbars=0");


改成


 var codeWin = window.open("/insertcode.aspx","codeWin","width=530,height=420,status=0,toolbars=0");
